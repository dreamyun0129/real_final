The
Production

At DREAM CAFE we love baking. We maintain the best of traditional techniques whilst embracing what modern technology and thinking has to offer. As a company we are focused, well trained and passionate about our products. Located in the heart of the city our state-of-the-art production facility is a culinary dream, spread across 1500 sq.m. Equipped with the latest technologies the facility boasts the highest quality and hygiene standards. Using this ultra modern facility and traditional recipes, our expert team of bakers continue to produce an exceptional range of wholesome breads, pastries and confectioneries.
